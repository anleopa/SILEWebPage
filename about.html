The team

SILE has been developed by the PROS Research Center at the Universitat Politècnica de València with the aim of providing a feasible solution to the challenges that the increasing amount of genomic data generation is posing to researchers.

With a strong research background and a broad experience on Information Systems, our team helps users to focus only on the information that is really relevant to achieve their goals, reducing the noise that the information overload causes and easing the decision making process in a clinical contex.
